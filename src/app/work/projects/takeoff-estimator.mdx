## Overview

Construction takeoffs — extracting quantities and measurements from project plans — were a slow, manual process. The Takeoff Estimator automates that workflow end to end: it reads project plans, recognizes symbols and text with OCR, parses documents, and produces automated quantity calculations that feed directly into estimation.

## Technical Highlights

- **OCR pipeline**: PaddleOCR-based recognition of symbols and labels on construction plans.
- **Document parsing**: extracts structure and measurements from project plan PDFs.
- **Automated quantity calculations**: turns parsed plan data into estimation-ready quantities.
- **Desktop deployment**: shipped as a cross-platform app with Tauri (Rust + Node.js).

## Outcome

Reduced manual estimation effort significantly and deployed as production-ready software used by both internal staff and external users.

## Technologies Used

- **JavaScript / Node.js**: application logic and pipeline orchestration
- **Rust + Tauri**: cross-platform desktop packaging
- **Python + PaddleOCR**: OCR and document recognition
