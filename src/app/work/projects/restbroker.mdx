## Overview

Restaurants constantly face short-notice staffing gaps, and temporary service workers need a reliable way to find shifts. RestBroker connects the two: an on-demand staffing platform where restaurants post needs and workers book shifts, with the surrounding operations built in.

## Technical Highlights

- **Booking system**: shift posting, matching, and confirmation between restaurants and workers.
- **POS workflow integration**: staffing data ties into the restaurant's point-of-sale operations.
- **Scheduling**: availability, shift calendars, and coverage planning in one place.
- **Unified platform**: one operational surface instead of separate staffing, scheduling, and POS tools.

## Technologies Used

- **Python**: backend services
- **JavaScript / Node.js**: platform and booking workflows
